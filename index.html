About Jason Plus Productions

Theater, Music & Vocal Excellence Through Active Participation

Jason Plus Productions believes in interaction, engagement, and active participation. We don't believe in passive consumption. Whether you're stepping into a vocal lesson, jamming with a live band, or shaping the narrative of an interactive musical‚Äîyou're not a spectator, you're a participant.

Founded by Jason Hackner, a vocalist and theater director, Jason Plus Productions creates spaces where students become confident performers, where audiences become co-creators, and where live music becomes a conversation between artist and participant. Every experience is designed for active involvement and transformation.

Our Mission: Empowering people through vocal mastery, authentic live performance, and interactive theater that demands participation, rewards engagement, and celebrates the power of doing‚Äînot just watching.
