Audition Preparation

Those auditioning should prepare:

  • 16–32 bars of a George Michael song or 80s/90s pop ballad that shows your range and emotional storytelling
  • Brief cold read from provided sides (emailed upon scheduling)
  • Dress comfortably for movement; auditions may include light choreography
Important: This is a contemporary story about relationships, scored with George Michael's music. George Michael is NOT a character in the show—this is not a biopic or tribute concert.

Cast Roles

Role Description
Jordan

Micah

 Charismatic but self-absorbed guest singer craving spotlight; stirs jealousy with bold flirtations—powerhouse tenor (GM range), flashy stage moves. Arc: Thrill-seeking crashes into consequences.
