What to Prepare

  • A 2-minute cut of any George Michael or Wham! song, or a contemporary pop song that showcases both vocal ability and acting nuance/storytelling
  • Choose material you connect with and have fun — we want to get a sense of your personality
  • A cappella is acceptable, but performing with a backing track is preferred
  • An accompanist will not be provided at initial auditions — bring your track or instrument as needed
  • Bring a headshot and resume if you have them

What to Expect

  • 15-minute individual audition slot
  • Please arrive 10 minutes early to check in
  • Potential cast members should be prepared to come back on the third day (Sunday) for callbacks
  • Callbacks will be announced Saturday evening — calls will be made first, then the list will be posted online

Callbacks

  • If called back, you may be asked to read sides and/or sing specific song sections
  • An accompanist will be provided on callback day

About the Show

  • Interactive theatre experience featuring the music of George Michael & Wham!
  • Dramedy format blending comedy, drama, and live musical performance
  • Debuting July 2026 in Charlotte, NC

Character Breakdown

Arrangements features the following roles. All roles require strong vocal ability and comfort with dramatic storytelling. This is a modern dramedy — not a tribute show. Performers inhabit original characters whose lives are scored by the music of George Michael & Wham!

George Michael Lead
The centerpiece vocalist and dramatic anchor of the show. This role requires a powerful, emotionally versatile voice capable of handling George Michael's iconic catalog — from soulful ballads to high-energy pop. Must convey charisma, vulnerability, and commanding stage presence. Strong acting ability is essential, as this character drives the story's emotional arc. Looking for someone who can embody the spirit of the music while bringing a fresh, authentic performance to the role.
Andrew Ridgeley
The charismatic counterpart and dramatic foil. This role calls for strong comedic timing, natural charm, and the ability to hold the stage alongside the lead. Vocal ability is important, but the emphasis is on personality, physicality, and the dynamic interplay between the two leads. Must be comfortable with both comedic and dramatic beats — this character brings levity, tension, and heart to the story.
Backup Singer 1
A featured vocal role with dramatic presence. Must blend powerfully with the ensemble while also delivering standout solo moments. Strong harmonizing skills and the ability to convey emotion through both voice and physicality. This role is integral to the musical tapestry and has key dramatic moments that require genuine acting chops.
Backup Singer 2
A featured vocal role that complements Backup Singer 1. Requires excellent vocal range, strong harmonizing ability, and confidence in both solo and ensemble performance. Must bring energy and authenticity to the stage. Dramatic involvement is woven throughout — this isn't just a singing role, it's a storytelling role.
Band Member
A multi-instrumentalist who doubles as a dramatic character within the show. Must be proficient on their primary instrument and comfortable performing live in a theatrical context. The band isn't in the pit — they're part of the world of the show. Acting ability and willingness to engage with the narrative are expected.
Ensemble / Dancer
A versatile performer who brings energy, movement, and presence to the production. Must be comfortable with choreography, improvisation, and audience interaction. Vocal ability is a plus. Ensemble members are visible throughout and are essential to creating the immersive, interactive atmosphere that defines the show.

Diversity & Inclusion

Jason Plus Productions is committed to building a cast and creative team that reflects the full spectrum of the community we serve. We strongly encourage submissions from performers of all races, ethnicities, gender identities, sexual orientations, abilities, ages, and body types.

Arrangements is a story rooted in queer experience and the music of an artist who lived boldly and authentically. We are dedicated to honoring that legacy by creating a space where every performer is seen, valued, and celebrated for exactly who they are.

All casting decisions are based on talent, authenticity, and the creative needs of the production. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability, age, sexual orientation, or gender identity.
